about us

Salomel Group is considered one of the world’s largest industrial leaders in the production of formica sheets, . It operates as part of a major industrial conglomerate with manufacturing facilities in Egypt, Lebanon, Italy, and Jordan, and commercial branches in Egypt, Lebanon, Italy, and Canada that support the group’s raw material supply and other operational needs.

The group began its operations in Egypt in 1982, and since then, it has remained the leading and most reliable company, committed to delivering high-quality products and driving continuous, positive transformation across its offerings.

With over 45 years of experience in the industry, Salomel Group operates across a total area of approximately 25,000 square meters, employing more than 500 highly motivated staff and workers, all dedicated to upholding quality and innovation.

our mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality products and services, while also ensuring that we operate in a sustainable and environmentally responsible manner.
